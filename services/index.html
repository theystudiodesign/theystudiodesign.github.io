How to work with THE’Y

Sprint 2–4 weeks

One focused problem, fixed scope. A naming sprint, a landing page, a pitch deck that has to land.

Signature 6–10 weeks

The full brand or website engagement — strategy through launch. Most of our work, most of our case studies.

Partner Ongoing

A retainer: THE’Y as your external design department. Priority access, compounding context.

On pricing

Every engagement is scoped to the problem, not the hour. Signature brand engagements typically start in the mid four figures (USD); we’ll give you a precise, fixed quote after one call — and we’ll tell you honestly if we’re not the right fit, or if a smaller sprint would serve you better.

The process, one level deeper

  1. 01 Discover

    What happens: stakeholder interviews, market and audience review, audit of everything you have.

    You get: a findings memo. We need: honesty and access.

  2. 02 Define

    What happens: positioning, strategy, and the brief distilled to one page you sign off.

    You get: the strategy document. We need: one decision-maker.

  3. 03 Design

    What happens: concepts, weekly working sessions, structured feedback rounds.

    You get: real work every week. We need: feedback within 48h.

  4. 04 Deliver

    What happens: production, guidelines, handover, launch support.

    You get: the complete system plus a working session with your team. We need: nothing — this part is on us.

Questions we actually get

How long does a typical engagement take? +

Sprints run 2–4 weeks; Signature engagements 6–10 weeks depending on scope. We set the calendar in the proposal and we hold it — deadlines are design.

How many revisions are included? +

Structured rounds, not unlimited laps: typically two per phase, plus a final polish pass. In practice, the weekly working sessions catch direction issues before they become “revisions.”

Who do you work with? +

Ambitious companies of any size that take design seriously as a business tool — from funded startups to established firms repositioning. Geography doesn’t matter; we work remotely with clients worldwide from Casablanca.

We’re pre-launch and don’t have everything figured out. Too early? +

Not necessarily — strategy is the first half of every engagement, and the Discover/Define phases exist precisely to sharpen what’s fuzzy. If it truly is too early, we’ll tell you on the first call and suggest what to do first.

Who owns the work? +

You do. Full IP transfer of the final system on final payment, with source files and guidelines. We retain only the right to publish the case study — which we’ll clear with you first.

How does remote collaboration work? +

A shared workspace, one weekly working session on video, and asynchronous reviews in between. You always see work-in-progress, never a big reveal at the end. Time zones have never been the problem; silence has — so we don’t go silent.

What don’t you do? +

Performance marketing, SEO retainers, video production at scale, and anything we can’t make excellent. When a project needs those, we bring in specialists we trust or point you to them directly.

Not sure which you need? That’s what the first call is for.
